Welcome to Quick guide

Useful tips are down below.

Department functional:

    Add department

  • If you want to create a department just follow the link in the navbar or click the departments. Then press the "Add new department" button. Fill in the required fields with your data then press "Add department" button and confirm your action by pressing "Add" button.

    • Edit department

  • If you want to edit a department just follow the link in the navbar or click the departments. There you will see a list of departments. Press the edit icon in row of the department you want to edit . Then fill the required fields with new data and press "Save Changes" button and confirm your action by pressing "Save Changes" button.

    • Delete department

  • If you want to delete a department just follow the link in the navbar or click the departments. There you will see a list of departments. Press the delete icon in row of the department you want to delete . Then just confirm your action by pressing "Delete department" button.

    • Useful tips

  • You can also go to the page of each department simply by clicking on its name. You can sort the list of departments as well by just clicking on one of the categories (department, organization, average salary).
